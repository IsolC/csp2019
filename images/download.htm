SDSC Apparel and Team Gear

Looking for SDSC Adidas team gear, fan wear, or soccer equipment such as cleats, sleeves, balls, bags, cones, jackets, warm up pants, or socks?

San Diego Soccer Club has teamed up with Adidas and Soccer Garage for our player’s uniforms, Club Gear, Fan Wear, and soccer gear. SDSC members and their families can order direct via Soccer Garage. 

Available SDSC Gear: SDSC team kit shorts, socks, tees, training tee, jackets, backpacks. Other gear available are long sleeve training tees, and warm up pants, goal keeper shorts and sock, base layer shorts; a variety of fan wear including hooded sweatshirts, polo shirts, women’s cut shirts, hats, and navy, gray and orange tees, and other adidas brand gear.

Instructions for placing the uniform order for your SDSC player can be found here.

If you need additional gear throughout the year, use the discount code SDSC15 for a 15% discount on your entire order. Your order will be shipped directly to your mailing address (initially uniform kits at the beginning of the season will be sent to team managers).
